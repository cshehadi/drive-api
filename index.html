Assignment: Character
Assignment #2 - Due Before 7/26

Knowing Your Characters

  1. Take the fiction piece (or idea) you're working on for Booth and, for the main character, come up with a list of qualities of the character you want to stress. Eg: moody, enthusiastic, combative.
  2. Then, for each quality, list five or more actions, phrases, descriptions or illuminating details that particularly demonstrate this quality for the character.
  3. Choose one action or detail, and write half a page (250 words) of a scene in which that action takes place, or that detail is described, for this character. If possible, try your hand at both showing and telling the character in this scene.

Turn in your lists of qualities, actions/details and the half-page scene.

Try to keep it under 500 words, with 750 words as a maximum.
